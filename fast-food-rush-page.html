Fast Food Rush

Test your speed and reflexes in this exciting fast food service game!

About Fast Food Rush

Fast Food Rush puts you in charge of a busy fast food restaurant. Customers are hungry and impatient - it's up to you to take their orders, prepare their meals, and serve them as quickly as possible. The faster you serve, the more points you earn. But be careful not to mix up the orders, or you'll lose valuable points!

This fast-paced game will test your memory, speed, and multitasking abilities. Can you handle the rush hour and become the ultimate fast food champion?

Game Features

  • Engaging fast-paced gameplay that tests your reflexes
  • Multiple levels with increasing difficulty
  • Variety of food items to prepare and serve
  • Colorful graphics and fun sound effects
  • Easy to learn, challenging to master

How to Play

Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game. Click on ingredients to prepare orders, drag items to assemble meals, and deliver them to the right customers before they lose patience.

Action Control
Select/Interact Left Mouse Click or Tap
Drag Items Click/Touch and Hold
Serve Order Drag Food to Customer
Pause Game ESC key or Menu Button
